Location

View Location Map
Le Yaudet is situated on the Cotes D'Armor, 50 minutes drive from Roscoff and 10 minutes west of Lannion.

 

 

The beach at Le Yaudet is within walking distance from the house.

It is accessed via steps from the headland.

It is very quiet and unspoilt (no shops or bars).

 
The area around Le Yaudet is perfect for walking and cycling.

There are many signposted coastal walks and paths to be explored.
