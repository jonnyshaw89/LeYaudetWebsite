Detached Neo Breton house set in the renowned beauty spot of Le Yaudet with it's little chapel, sandy coves and superb views of the coastline.

The house is 5 minutes walk from the headland and the coastline in this area is at times dramatic and at times lush and beautiful. A short drive takes you to the beaches of Beg Leguer and the other beautiful sandy beaches of the Pink Granite coast. There are also many local towns and villages to explore nearby which are very interesting to visit.

Lannion 10 minutes drive with its excellent shops, large Thursday street market and numerous restaurants.

Fishing, riding, good walking,cycling, land yachting and sailing are all available in the local area.In the garage there are 4 bikes (Gents, Ladies and two childs) on which one can explore the countryside. 

Ground Floor: Lounge/Diner with French doors opening onto large terrace and garden. Fully equipped kitchen including Dishwasher, Fridge/Freezer, Oven and Microwave. Utility room with washing machine. WC with washbasin.

First Floor: 3 Bedrooms (1 Double, 1 Twin and 1 with Bunk beds), Bathroom with toilet and shower over bath.

The house now benefits from having Free Sat TV which receives a very wide range of English TV channels. We also now have 3 child stair gates, Travel cot and highchair

Electric heating.

Large lawned garden to the rear with second patio area under the trees. Private gravelled driveway for 3 cars. Garden furniture, BBQ and Bikes
